General Interest/Area of Focus

Lenna Nash completed her M.A. in Anthropology at the University of Mississippi in 2010. Her M.A. thesis was entitled: “Osteology of the Chalillo Dam Archaeology Salvage Project, Upper Macal River Valley, Belize.”

Lenna has several years experience in archaeological survey, excavation, and lab processing of archaeological materials and osteological remains, including the excavation and analysis of Mayan skeletal remains in Belize. Nash also has experience with several large cemetery relocation projects in Atlanta, Georgia and Charleston, South Carolina.
