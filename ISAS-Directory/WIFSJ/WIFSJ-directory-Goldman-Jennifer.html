General Interest/Area of Focus

Jennifer Goldman has been working at ISAS for over 2 years. Her interests include prehistoric burial mounds and historic cemeteries, prehistoric lithic assemblages, osteological studies, and artifact photography/editing. She has experience in field data collection, lab processing (including washing, labeling, curating, artifact inventory, generalized research, etc.), IAS form completion, map production, use of Trimble GPS receivers, ArcMap/GIS, GPS Pathfinder, and Photoshop.
