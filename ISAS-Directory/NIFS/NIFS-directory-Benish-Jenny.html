General Interest/Area of Focus

Jenny Benish started employment at the Northern Illinois Field Station at ISAS as an Archaeological Specialist and progressed to a Staff Archaeologist in 2013. Jenny has performed archaeological investigations in many states (KY, TN, LA, MO, IL, WI, and AR); however, her current primary focus is Midwest historic and prehistoric archaeology.

Current Research

Analysis of the Johnson Collection, a private assemblage of prehistoric material from a series of sites in Illinois and Wisconsin with temporal affiliations ranging from Paleo-Indian to Mississippian.

Development of a comparative collection for historic archaeological materials including domestic, construction, and personal artifacts.
