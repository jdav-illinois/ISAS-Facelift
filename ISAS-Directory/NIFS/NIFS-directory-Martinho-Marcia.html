General Interest/Area of Focus

Anthropological/Archaeological Illustration with a focus on Midwestern North American cultures and their artifacts. Marcia L. Martinho is a skilled illustrator and photographer, whose work has contributed to many reports and research presentations, over the course of a ten-year career.
