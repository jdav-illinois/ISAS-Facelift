General Interest/Area of Focus

Lauren Fitts has been working at ISAS for 3 years. Her interests and professional focus center on prehistoric lithic assemblages, Archaic cultures and historic Euro-American cultural material. She has experience in ArcGIS and is proficient in all manner of field data collection employing GPS and total station technologies.
